About the Program

Greek Empire is a custom apparel company built specifically for sororities and fraternities. As a Campus Ambassador, you'll help connect chapters at your school with Greek Empire while gaining experience in sales, marketing, networking, and entrepreneurship. You'll build relationships on campus, help organizations create custom merchandise, and earn commission on orders you bring in.

What We Provide
01

Triple earning potential

Earn through chapter orders, social media content, and ambassador referrals. Make your own hours — most ambassadors spend 1–4 hrs/week.

02

Dedicated support

Our team handles design, production, and order management. You focus on building relationships and growing Greek Empire on campus.

03

Personalized marketing plan

Access marketing materials, content ideas, and promotional support to reach more students and organizations.

04

Free gear

Receive exclusive Greek Empire apparel and product samples to wear and promote on campus.

05

Early access

Be the first to see and promote new products, collections, and limited releases.

06

Real experience

Develop skills in sales, marketing, communication, networking, and brand building while helping grow a startup.

3 Ways to Earn
01

Commission on orders

Earn 10% on every order placed using your referral code. No cap. Every chapter you refer also earns 2.5%–5% back on qualifying orders.

02

Social Media Incentive (SIP)

Earn bonuses by creating approved content on TikTok and Instagram. Submit through the Greek Empire content portal before posting.

03

Ambassador referrals

Earn 2.5% commission when qualified students you refer join the Campus Ambassador Program.

What We Expect
  • Build relationships with sororities and fraternities on your campus.
  • Connect chapter leaders with Greek Empire for custom apparel and merchandise.
  • Stay responsive and communicate regularly with the Greek Empire team.
  • Help organize and manage chapter orders with support from our team.
  • Actively promote Greek Empire merch to chapter leadership — Merch Chairs, Rush Chairs, and Social Chairs.
  • Generate social content to earn more from our Social Media Incentive Program (Instagram and/or TikTok).
  • Represent Greek Empire authentically within your chapter and campus community.
  • Greek Empire limits ambassadors per school — work hard to keep your spot.
  • Log all chapter contacts and orders in the Greek Empire CRM.
  • Conduct yourself in a way consistent with the Greek Empire brand — elevated, credible, and culturally embedded.
